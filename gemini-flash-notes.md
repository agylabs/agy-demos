# Google Gemini 3.5 Flash Notes

## 📅 Release Information
*   **Release Date:** May 19, 2026 (announced at Google I/O 2026)
*   **Availability:** Released directly to general availability across Google consumer services (Gemini app, AI Mode in Google Search), developer portals (Google AI Studio, Android Studio, Antigravity platform), and enterprise integrations.

---

## ⚡ Key Improvements over Previous Models

### 1. Enhanced Agentic Capabilities
*   Optimized specifically for complex, long-horizon agentic tasks.
*   Shows superior performance in multi-step workflows, parallel agent execution, and autonomous decision-making.

### 2. Advanced Coding Proficiency
*   Demonstrates notable gains in software engineering tasks.
*   Outperforms prior iterations (such as Gemini 3.1 Pro) on core programming and developer-centric benchmarks (e.g., Terminal-Bench 2.1).

### 3. Greater Accuracy & Reasoning Quality
*   Achieves higher scores on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index compared to Gemini 3 Flash.
*   Features enhanced logical reasoning power accompanied by a significant reduction in hallucination rates.

### 4. Native Multimodal Support
*   Advanced understanding and reasoning across multi-format inputs: Text, Images, Videos, Audios, and PDF documents.

### 5. Configurable "Thinking" Controls
*   Supports developer-controlled "thinking levels" (Minimal, Low, Medium, High) to balance answer depth, latency, and operational cost.

### 6. Speed & Window Capabilities
*   Highly optimized for low latency, achieving speeds of over 280 output tokens per second.
*   Supports a massive **1-Million token context window** and up to **65,536 output tokens**.

### 7. Frontier Safety Framework
*   Built under Google's Frontier Safety Framework, adding robust safeguards against harmful content.
*   Integrates interpretability tools that allow analyzing the model's internal reasoning process prior to output generation.
